Frequently Asked Questions
What are Value Markets?+
Value markets are statistical betting options most people overlook. They analyze specific in-game events like Yellow Cards, No Brace, or Ball Not Hitting Post. This gives you an edge because fewer people analyze them — better odds, higher win rates.
What are Main Markets?+
Main markets are the popular betting options most people use: Match Winner (1X2), Over/Under 2.5 Goals, and BTTS. Easy to understand but highly competitive and often less predictable over time.
